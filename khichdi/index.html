Simple Khichdi

Ingredients

  • 1/2c Rice (Royal Chef's Secret Extra Long Basmati Rice)
  • 1/2c Split Green Lentils (Laxmi brand Moong Dal)
  • 1 TB butter (Kerrygold unsalted)
  • 1/4 tsp ground blackpepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp whole cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Combine rice(1/2 cup) w/ lentils(1/2 cup) and soak for 15 minutes to overnight.
  2. Warm butter(1TB) in a small pot on med-low (30%) until the water from butter evaporates and you begin to hear a crackling.
  3. Add cumin(1tsp) and toast for ~1 min or until fragrant.
  4. Drain rice/lentil mixture and add to the pot.
  5. Add ground black pepper(1/4 tsp), turmeric(1/4 tsp), salt(1 tsp), water(3 cups), and turn the heat up to 100% for 5 minutes, covered.
  6. Lower heat to 20% (about 2) and simmer for 25 minutes, covered.
  7. Add salt to taste, and enjoy!
