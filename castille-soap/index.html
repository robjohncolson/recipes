Homemade Castille Soap

Wear gloves, clothes and goggles. You are using lye. Use at warning, lye interacting with water can burn skin.

Items


  • 58 g NaOH (Sodium Hydroxide/lye)
  • 454 g Olive Oil
  • 4 g lemongrass essential oil (or your choice, optional)

Steps

  1. Do this step in the sink. With protective gear on, carefully pour water into *stainless* steel bowl. (Do not use aluminum, glass or plastic bowls)
  2. Add NaOH, 4 g salt to water.
  3. Stir the alkali solution (in the sink, DO NOT splash).
  4. Make sure the mixture is mixed, DO NOT splash.
  5. Add essential oil to lye mixture. Stir, DO NOT splash.
  6. Add olive oil to lye mixture. Stir, DO NOT splash
  7. Take mixture, and blend in a blender til well it traces (gets thick), 30 seconds.
  8. Place back in *stainless* steel bowl. Let sit in cool dark space 48 hours - 1 week.

Yields 6 bars of soap

Can remove from bowl in 48 hours - 1 week or more

Ready to officially use in 4 weeks for optimal soap.
