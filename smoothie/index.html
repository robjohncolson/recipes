BananaProteinSHake

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 80 g raw whey
  • 2/3 C frozen blueberries
  • 1tsp beef gelatin
  • 5 bananas
    • 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. peel the bananas and stick in blender
    2. put in ~80g of whey protein powder
  2. add 1tsp gelatin
  3. add blueberries
  4. add water
  5. blend
  6. seaparate 6 eggs, reserve the whites and stick in the fridge for using in chinese egg-white dessert recip.
  7. add egg yolks, blend, then serve in two large cups
