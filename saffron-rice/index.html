Saffron Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp saffron
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Simmer water & rice on med-high (setting 7) without lid for 6 minutes.
  2. When surface is bubbly & foamy, turn to medium-low (setting 4). Put on lid for 12 minutes.
  3. Add saffron, salt & olive oil. Reduce temperature to low (setting 2). Cook for 35 minutes.
  4. Serve!
