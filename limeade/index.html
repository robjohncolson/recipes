Homemade Ginger Limeade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh squeezed lime juice (~34 key limes or use regular)
  • heaping 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup drinking water
  • 6 nubs ginger
  • Extra water for blending ginger

Instructions

  1. Melt sugar with water on low (1-2 setting) on stovetop in ceramic pot until dissolved, stirring occassionally
  2. While sugar is melting, squeeze limes for lime juice, filtering out pits and pulp.
  3. In a blender, blend ginger, adding enough water to make liquid pulp.
  4. Using a fine mesh strainer, squeeze ginger juice into lime juice.
  5. Add sugar water to ginger and lime juice.
  6. Chill and serve in small cups.

Drink is concentrated. Makes ~6 small cups (6 fl oz)
