Simple Green Pea Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot
  • 1 bag frozen (or fresh) greenpeas (10 oz)
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 Tbsp all-purpose flour

Steps

  • Cook shallots with butter in ceramic pot, until appears clear/transluscent.
  • Add flour, stirring about 30 seconds or more. Don't overcook.
  • Add green peas and salt for ~5 minutes.
  • In a blender, blend until smooth and serve.
