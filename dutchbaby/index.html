Dutch Baby

Ingredients

  • 210 g ap flour
  • 3TB sugar
  • 1TB lemon zest (2 lemons)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 c milk
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 TB butter

Instructions

  1. mix dry ingredients
  2. mix wet
  3. combine
  4. melt butter in skillet
  5. pur batter into skillet
  6. put skillet into cold oven, and bring temperature up to 375
  7. done in 30 -35 min
