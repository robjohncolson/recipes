Use soap nuts to replace soap from...
  • your bathroom and kitchen.
  • your shower or bath
  • your washing machine
  • your dishwashing

Buy deseeded soap nuts from reputable sources such as Mountain Rose Herbs. They ship from another source in India, so it may take a long time to ship.

    Steps
  • Add ~15 soap nuts per quart of water, cook on stovetop on low about 30-40 minutes.
  • Take off stovetop and let cool down.
  • Pour in large glass container, filter out soap nuts but keep for future uses.
  • Optionally, add 15 drops rosemary oil.
  • Suggested to let rosemary soap nut liquid to sit in fridge for a day
  • Pour in squeezeable bottles to use in bath or sink. We use small succulant squeeze bottles for the soap which works well.
  • Keep soap bottles in fridge. Keep reusable soap nuts in fridge for the next batch.
  • Can recook soap nuts for about 2-3 more uses.

    • Note: if soap nut mixture begins to smell sour, discard. Soaked soap nuts are perishable as they are natural.
