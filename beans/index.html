Bean Recipe

Prepare in Advance

Soak 1 1/2 cup dry northern beans in water overnight. Drain and rinse the next day for use.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup northern beans (dry)
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 4 key limes or 1 regular lime
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 red onion

Instructions

  1. Simmer rinsed beans on low (setting 2) with lid for 1- 1 1/2 hours depending on softness preference.
  2. Let cool about 30 minutes. Add salt and stir.
  3. Serve beans in bowls, add squeezed limes and 1/2 chopped red onion.
  4. Serve! Pairs well with saffron rice.
