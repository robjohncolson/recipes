Irish Soda Bread

from Sally's Baking Recipes

Ingredients
  • 1 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 4 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 Tbsp white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 5 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 cup raisins
Instructions

Prepare in advance option for higher malt flavor: soak 1 cup raisins in whiskey

  1. Pre-heat oven at 400 degrees F.
  2. Grease and lightly flour cast-iron skillet
  3. Mix all ingredients together in large glass bowl until well blended; pour into skillet.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees F for ~50 minutes or until top is brown. If top starts to brown too much, tent lightly with aluminium foil.
  5. Be sure center is cooked by tapping center to ensure it is more hollow than doughy.
