Cornpop Recipe

Ingredients

For the cornpop dough:
  • 300 grams of starter dough
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2/3 cup AP flour
  • 1/3 Cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup sugar?

    • NOTE: measurements are currently guesses!!!

For the date-butter:
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 pitted dates
  • 1/8 tea salt

Instructions

  1. Pour water in large glass bowl and add flour, cornmeal and sugar; blend then knead with hands until doughy consistency. Add dough as needed.
  2. Knead in starter dough to fresh dough. Knead about one minute.
  3. Lightly oil (olive oil) bottom of dutch oven.
  4. Let sit for one hour or more, then place in dutch oven on stovetop setting 3.2 (low) for 30 minutes.
  5. While dough is cooking, with a fork or blender, mash dates with butter and salt until as smooth as possible.
  6. Take out cooked dough, lightly cover bottom of dutch oven, cook bottom of dough at 3.2 for 22 minutes.
  7. Bon appetit!
