2lb mash potato

Ingredients

  • 2lb yellow potato
  • 1.5 stick butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. peel potato, cube and put into a pot with approx 1 inch of water (not much)
  2. bring pot to a boil (takes ~13 min on low)
  3. boil/steam the potatoes for ~20 min
  4. cut up the 4oz of butter into ~8 pieces and put into a mixer
  5. strain cooked potatos and put into mixer with a suitable paddle
  6. add salt, pepper, and mix until you feel good.
