Dutch Oven Bread Recipe on the Stovetop

Steps for Starter Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 200 mg (~1 1/2 cup) water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Mix dry yeast and sugar into water to 'proof stirring until dissolved
  2. Add flours and olive oil into water mixture with a spatula. Knead dough until doughy consistency, about a minute and add flour as needed, with clean hands
  3. Let dough rise about 4 hours in covered glass bowl in fridge, up to overnight
  4. If using only as starter dough, continue to "Steps to Use Starter Dough"
  5. Otherwise, bake dough in dutch oven on stovetop 30 minutes on setting 3.2. Flip for 25 minutes then serve.

    6. Starter batch yields one large loaf.

Steps to Use Starter Dough

Ingredients (ultimately doubling the recipe, without yeast, adding some salt.

  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp or less salt
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Add sugar and salt into water and stir until dissolved
  2. Add flours into water mixture, with a spatula. Work dough until doughy consistency with clean hands.
  3. Add starter dough to fresh dough, being careful as it may stick to the glass bowl.
  4. Knead dough for a few minutes, adding flour as needed to keep dough consistency.
  5. Let dough rise ~4 hours in glass container. Cover dough with olive oil to prevent sticking from glass. Up to 24 hours stays well.
  6. When ready, separate dough in half. Place one half in glass bowl to use as starter dough.
  7. Lightly oil dutch oven bottom. Salt top and bottom of new dough and place in dutch oven to rise a few hours.
  8. Bake dough in dutch oven on stovetop 30 minutes on setting 3.2. Flip for 25 minutes, re-oiling the bottom of dutch oven, then serve.
