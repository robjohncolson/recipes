Garlic Parsley Olive Oil Dip Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 tsp salt
  • 6 large bulbs garlic
  • 1 bunch curly leaf Italian parsley

Instructions

  1. Combine salt and olive oil
  2. Mince garlic and add to olive oil mixture.
  3. Pluck parsley leaves and mince. Add to mixture
  4. Serve! Works well as a dip for fresh bread with tahini on the side.

14 servings
