Egg White Dessert Recipe

Ingredients

  • 24 egg whites
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 8 TB sugar
  • 1/2 tsp almond essence

Instructions

  1. blend/whisk the egg whites to break apart their structure, but try not to make it too bubbly
  2. warm milk in microwave until warmer than your body temperature
  3. strain the egg whites into a bowl, then blend/whisk warm milk, almond essence and sugar.
  4. strain into bowl with egg white. Steam for 15 minutes, and then let rest for 10
