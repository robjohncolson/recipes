Lebanese Sandwich

Prepare White Bean Sauce in advance!

Make flatbread a few hours before, or bake while making sandwich filling.

Ingredients
for the chimmichuri yay delish
  • 2/3 oz fresh mint
  • 1/3 bunch curly parsley
  • 1 small bulb garlic
  • 6 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
salad part 1
  • 1 knob (~1 gram) tumeric
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
dressing
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 keylimes
  • 12 peppercorns (1/8 teaspoon ground)
  • 1 pinch saffron
salad part 2
  • 1/2 bulb small red onion
  • 2 small heads romaine lettuce (we get from package or one large romaine lettuce head)
