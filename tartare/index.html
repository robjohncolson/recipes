Raw meatloaf

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hamburger meat
  • 1 TB capers
  • 1 tsp beef gelatin powder
  • 1/3 vidalia onion
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 5 dash tabasco
  • 1 tsp worcestshire sauce
  • 1/8 c chopped parsley
  • 1/4 tsp dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. mix
  2. shape into loaf
  3. garnish with capers and onions.
